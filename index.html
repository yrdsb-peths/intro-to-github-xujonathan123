Who We Are

Young Sparks STEM was started by a group of high school students in 2025. The team focuses on designing, building, and experimenting with projects that address real-world challenges, including environmental issues. Through hands-on projects, problem-solving, and creative experimentation, Young Sparks STEM aims to develop practical engineering skills, explore innovative ideas, and learn how to tackle complex problems collaboratively.

Our Projects

Explore our engineering projects, where we experiment with creative ideas and build hands-on solutions. Each project focuses on applying practical skills and problem-solving techniques to real-world challenges, while also testing new concepts and designs.

Our Team

Meet the people behind our team who handle the planning, building, and testing of our projects. Each member focuses on different aspects, from design to hands-on construction, working together to complete our engineering challenges.
